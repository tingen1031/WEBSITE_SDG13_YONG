About the Event

The Climate Action Hackathon 2025 brings together students, developers, designers, and innovators to tackle urban climate challenges like heat and floods. Participants will build digital prototypes, visual tools, and community apps that promote climate resilience.

SDG 13 — Climate Action

This hackathon supports Sustainable Development Goal 13 by promoting awareness and innovation to reduce climate risks through green infrastructure mapping, flood early-warning systems, and cooling strategies.
